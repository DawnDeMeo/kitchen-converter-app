Privacy Policy for Kitchen Unit Converter

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

Overview

Kitchen Unit Converter ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The App does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Use analytics or advertising services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Require you to create an account

Data Storage

All data created in the App is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Custom ingredients you create
  • Your favorite ingredients
  • App preferences and settings
  • Recently used ingredients

iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync on your device, the App uses Apple's CloudKit service to synchronize your data across your devices. This data includes:

  • Custom ingredients
  • Favorites
  • App preferences

When using iCloud sync:

  • Data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only you have access to this data
  • We (the app developers) cannot access your iCloud data
  • iCloud sync is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • iCloud: To sync your data across your devices (optional)

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

All data is stored using Apple's secure storage frameworks (SwiftData and CloudKit). We take data security seriously and follow Apple's best practices for data protection.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete custom ingredients at any time within the App
  • You can export your custom ingredients as a backup
  • You can disable iCloud sync in your device Settings
  • You can delete the App and all associated data by removing it from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Default Ingredient Database

The App includes a curated database of common cooking ingredients with verified conversion data. This database:

  • Is bundled with the App
  • Does not track which ingredients you use
  • Sources data from public databases (USDA FoodData Central, King Arthur Baking)
  • Is stored locally on your device
